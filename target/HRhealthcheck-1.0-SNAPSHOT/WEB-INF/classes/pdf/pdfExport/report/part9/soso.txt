<p>A key component of engagement is reward. Research conducted by the Chartered Institute of Payroll Professionals shows 85 per cent of workers rate flexible employee benefits as either “very important” or “important” to them. Often, though, the financial value of the benefits package is underestimated by employees, meaning employers need to put more effort into educating staff about just what it is they receive in addition to their basic salary. What’s more,successful communications have to make an impact. People receive hundreds of marketing messages every day and they make snap decisions on what to review, ignore or delete. The “EASY” communication model can help guide the content creation process to create more meaningful and accessible messaging.</p>
